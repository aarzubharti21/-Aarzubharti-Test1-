Project Overview

This project analyzes Amazon sales data to uncover business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, inventory management, fulfillment operations, and customer satisfaction.

The analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel, including:

Data Cleaning
Pivot Tables
Pivot Charts
KPI Cards
Slicers
Timeline Filters
Interactive Dashboard

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

Business Objectives:

The project addresses four major business areas:

1. Customer Insights Analysis
Identify top-performing locations based on sales.
Analyze cancellation trends.
Compare B2B and B2C customer behavior.
Support targeted

2.FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS
Analyzing total revenue growth over time.
Identifying best-selling products and
seasonal sales trends.
Calculating the average order value to
understand spending behavior.marketing initiatives.

3.Product Management & Inventory Optimization
Identify high-demand product categories.
Analyze quantity ordered.
Support inventory planning.
Reduce stockout and overstock risks.

4. Logistics & Fulfillment Optimization
Compare Amazon vs Merchant fulfillment.
Analyze order status distribution.
Evaluate fulfillment efficiency.
Improve operational performance.

5. Customer Satisfaction & Returns
Investigate cancellation rates.
Identify problematic product categories.
Improve customer experience.
Recommend service improvements.
